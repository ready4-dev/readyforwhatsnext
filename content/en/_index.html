readyforwhatsnext is being developed to explore multiple economic questions in youth mental health using R. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="dark">}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-heartbeat" title="Health and its value" %}} Tools to value youth mental health outcomes through the [construction](/docs/tutorials/using-modules/map-to-utility/) and [use](/docs/tutorials/using-modules/predict-utility/) of utility mapping models. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-arrows-alt" title="Determinants of health" %}} [Synthetic populations](/docs/getting-started/pipeline/pipeline-people/) characterising the risk and protective factors for mental disorders. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-chart-area" title="Demand for healthcare" %}} [Spatial epidemiology](/docs/getting-started/model/places/) and [helpseeking choice](/docs/tutorials/using-modules/predict-choice/) models. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-chart-line" title="Supply of healthcare" %}} Models of [primary and specialist youth mental health services](/docs/getting-started/pipeline/pipeline-platforms/). {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-people-carry" title="Efficiency and equity" %}} Models to assess distributional impacts and optimal targeting of [youth mental health care provision](/docs/getting-started/pipeline/pipeline-platforms/). {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-comment-dollar" title="Economic evaluation" %}} [Tools for evaluating](/docs/getting-started/model/programs/) competing youth mental health policy and system design options. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}}
