readyforwhatsnext is being developed to explore multiple economic questions in youth mental health. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="dark">}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-heartbeat" title="Health and its value" %}} Valuation of youth mental health outcomes through the [development](/docs/model/modules/using-modules/people/map-to-utility/) and [use](/docs/model/modules/using-modules/people/predict-utility/) of utility mapping models. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-arrows-alt" title="Determinants of health" %}} [Synthetic populations with risk and protective factors](/docs/model/modules/pipeline/pipeline-people/) for mental disorders. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-chart-area" title="Demand for healthcare" %}} [Spatial epidemiology](/docs/model/modules/using-modules/places/) and [helpseeking choice](/docs/model/modules/using-modules/people/predict-choice/) models. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-chart-line" title="Supply of healthcare" %}} Models of [primary and specialist youth mental health services](/docs/model/modules/pipeline/pipeline-platforms/). {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-people-carry" title="Efficiency and equity" %}} Models to assess distributional impacts and optimal targeting of [youth mental health care provision](/docs/model/modules/pipeline/pipeline-platforms/). {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fas fa-comment-dollar" title="Economic evaluation" %}} Models to assess the [budget impact](/docs/model/modules/pipeline/pipeline-programs/) and [cost-utility](/docs/model/modules/using-modules/people/assess-cost-utility/) of competing youth mental health policy and system design options. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}}
